About This Portfolio

Welcome to my Communication Lab Portfolio. Here you can find a collection of my work and projects. This portfolio is a reflection of my work I have completed throughout this semester.

Assignment 1: 30MFF Web Debut

This assignment involved creating a web page for the 30MFF, focusing on branding and interactivity. The project included thoughtful consideration of story, title, tagline, fonts, colors, and embedding videos with interactive elements like photo carousels and sliders.

Visit Assignment 1

Assignment 2: Interactive Digital Comic

For this assignment, I created a 6-panel interactive comic using 2D software, incorporating original drawings, photographs, and mixed media. The project focused on storytelling and interactivity, including features like sound, animation, and 'choose your own adventure' elements.

Visit Assignment 2

Assignment 3: Sonic Journey

Assignment 3 was a 2-3 minute sound piece structured as a story with a beginning, middle, and end. The piece included originally recorded audio and was hosted on a web page that captured the theme and tone of the audio, offering an immersive experience.

Visit Assignment 3

Assignment 4: Storytelling Through Video

This project involved creating a 4-6 minute video piece with a clear narrative and identifiable characters. The video, made with original content, demonstrated editing and effects skills, and was integrated into an interactive web experience.
